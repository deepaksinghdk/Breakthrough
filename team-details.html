About Rejith

An ICCA Level 3 cricket coach and Ph.D. holder in physical education and sports science, serves as the Head Consultant for Breakthrough Personal Sport Coaching Services. A dynamic coach and educator, he helps schools, clubs, and academies equip students with essential skills like creativity, resilience, teamwork, and decision-making to reach their full potential, both physically and mentally.

Passionate about providing opportunities for youth to engage in competitive sports, Rejith also supports colleagues in developing innovative practices for curricula and assessments. With extensive experience leading PE in primary and secondary sectors, as well as serving as Head of the Secondary School Department and Lead Coach at ICC Academy, he understands what makes an exceptional coaching session and physical education class.
